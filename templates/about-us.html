We are a group of Computer Science and Engineering students from Techno International New Town, Kolkata. Our common interest in solving real-life problems with the help of artificial intelligence led us to realization of this project. Mental illness is still considered a taboo in large factions of our society despite it being so common in todays' age. Our team thinks that we can solve this problem with awareness and being cognizant of own mental condition. By leveraging our interests in AI, front and backend application development; we are offering you this safe and privet space where we listen to you and help you get though your difficulties.
